About us

A place shaped by faith and family.

Casa Del Alfarero means the Potter's House. Our heart is to welcome people into a community where lives are restored, families are strengthened, and faith becomes part of everyday life.

Whether you are visiting for the first time or looking for a church home in Tampa, there is a seat for you here.

Worship Christ-centered gatherings with prayer and praise.
